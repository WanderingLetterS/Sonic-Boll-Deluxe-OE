# Sonic Boll Deluxe (OE)

Boll Deluxe is a fangame featuring Super Mario and Sonic the Hedgehog, with an emphasis on modding and customization.

Sonic Boll Deluxe has sunsetted development. Some of us at Boll Team will still maintain this game, and potentially still even add stuff to it, along with accepting Pull Requests, but development from the official Boll Team will be little to none. At least for now.

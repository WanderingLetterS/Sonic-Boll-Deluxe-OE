[Gameplay Changes]
- New move "Piko Ball", inspired by Antonblast and the Sonic 3 AIR Extra Slot Project/Framework
- Added a wall jump, also inspired by the S3AIR mods
- Hammer Flip has been removed
- Dive/Trip has been removed
- Hammer Twirl has been re-mapped to the B button, and is now a short dash sideways
- Hammer Twirl can now be used on the ground if at running speed
- Hammer Twirl now knocks enemies away
- Hammer Swing now gives Amy partial invincibility to make it safer
- Hammer Swing now stomps enemies, if possible
- New versions of the Up+B and Down+B moves from older versions
- Hammer Drop / Slam is now "coin explosive" (breaking many coins out from multi-coin boxes)
- Most hammer moves can now break cracked ground
- Skip has been re-added
- Slide kick has been re-added, and its recovery jump now skips
- Amy can now roll out of slide kick

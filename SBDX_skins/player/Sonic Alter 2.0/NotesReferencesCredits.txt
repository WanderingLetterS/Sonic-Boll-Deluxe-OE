So, I saw Simple and I was like, "hey, your hairdo is fire, what if Sonic had it?" and then it all spiraled down
from there. I could've just made a personal recolor and went about my day, but then I had to go crazy and stupid.
What kind of hedgehog have I created? The design feels like Sonic, but some kind of
alternative universe Sonic. I guess I'll call him Sonic Alter.

-GoldenB4, a guy spriting a blue fuzzball at 4 A.M.
